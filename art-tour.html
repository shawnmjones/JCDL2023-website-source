Santa Fe Art Tours

For JCDL attendees, we are offering 20% off of tours booked on June 30 and July 1, 2023. Enter promo code JCDL when booking your tour online at santafearttours.com or call us at 505-985-5298.

Curious about the art and culture of Santa Fe? Take a tour with Santa Fe Art Tours to experience the best of Santa Fe's incredible art scene. On our walking tours, explore charming neighborhoods, hidden courtyards, off-the-beaten path art venues, and landmark galleries. You'll discover the stories behind the artwork, pick up tips for looking at art with an inquisitive eye, chat with local artists, and learn about the history and culture of Santa Fe through its thriving art scene.
