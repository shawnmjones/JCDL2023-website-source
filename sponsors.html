JCDL 2023 Sponsorship Opportunities

“Rethinking Digital Records” Exploring new perspectives, challenges, and opportunities for libraries, archives, museums, and galleries

The ACM/IEEE Joint Conference on Digital Libraries (JCDL 2023) represents a significant opportunity to reach an international community of computer-, information-, and data scientists as well as practitioners, librarians, and archivists engaged in the development, management, and application of digital libraries and associated issues.

JCDL offers three sponsorship levels and various targeted sponsorship opportunities:

  1. Red Chili Level: $1,000+
    Sponsors receive:
    • Small logo or name on the conference website and online program (linked to the organization’s website) as Red Chili Sponsor
    • Acknowledgement during opening and closing session as Red Chili Sponsor
    • Brochure in conference package
  2. Green Chili Level: $2,500+
    Sponsors receive:
    • Medium logo or name on the conference website and online program (linked to the organization’s website) as Green Chili Sponsor
    • Acknowledgement during opening and closing session as Green Chili Sponsor
    • Brochure in conference package
    • 1 conference registrations
  3. Red & Green Chili Level (aka “Christmas”): $5,000+
    Sponsors receive:
    • Large logo or name on the conference website and online program (linked to the organization’s website) as Red & Green Chili Sponsor
    • Acknowledgement during opening and closing session as Red & Green Chili Sponsor
    • Brochure in conference package
    • 2 conference registrations
    • Signage for brand awareness at conference

Targeted sponsorship opportunities:

Please contact the general chair Martin Klein (mailto:mklein@lanl.gov) for targeted sponsorship opportunities:

  • Vannevar Bush best paper award sponsorship and recognition
  • Best student paper award sponsorship and recognition
  • Keynote and speaker fees
  • Reception sponsorship
  • Banquet dinner sponsorship
  • Morning and/or afternoon break sponsorship
  • Live captioning of conference sessions
