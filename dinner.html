Conference Dinner

The conference dinner will be held on June 28, 2023 at La Fonda on the Plaza, located at:

100 E. San Francisco St.
Santa Fe NM
+1 800-523-5002

La Fonda is located on the corner of Cathedral Place and East San Francisco St. across from the Cathedral Basilica of St. Francis of Assisi and the IAIA Museum of Contemporary Native Arts. It is a 6-minute walk from the Hilton Santa Fe Historic Plaza, the Conference Hotel. Just walk straight down Water St. or East San Francisco St. from the Hilton toward the Cathedral.
