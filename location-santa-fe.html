Santa Fe

Santa Fe is the capital of the state of New Mexico, also called the land of enchantment. The city is the oldest state capital in the United States with a population of about 87,500. Santa Fe is world-renown for its vibrant art market with more than 100 galleries on Canyon Road alone. Further cultural highlights include the Santa Fe Plaza, the Georgia O'Keeffe Museum, Meow Wolf, and the famous Santa Fe Opera.

Santa Fe and the state of New Mexico are famous for their chile. Whether red, green, or red and green (commonly referred to as “christmas”), the traditional cuisine has something for everyone.
